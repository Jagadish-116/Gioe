Vision & Mission of GIOE

Driving excellence in technology education and research since 1998.

Our History

Founded with the mandate to serve the nation's growing electronic sector, GIOE has consistently been ranked among the top technical institutes. Our focus is on practical, industry-relevant knowledge.

Core Values

Innovation, Integrity, Excellence, and Inclusivity. These values guide our academic programs and community engagement, ensuring a holistic development environment for all students.

Accreditations

GIOE is recognized by the Ministry of Education and is fully accredited by the National Board of Accreditation (NBA) for all core engineering programs.

The GIOE Advantage

We boast state-of-the-art lab facilities, a highly qualified faculty, and strong industrial ties, resulting in a **95% placement rate** for graduating batches. Our graduates are industry-ready innovators.
